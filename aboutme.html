About me

Data analysis has become a natural extension of my work in IT support, where I’ve spent the past four years analyzing and tracking mobile app incidents in JIRA and ServiceNow. Through this experience, I developed a strong attention to detail and a habit of looking for patterns in data to identify broader issues early on.

I enjoy the technical side of data work — cleaning datasets, writing SQL queries, and visualizing results in Excel or Python — and I’m now focused on growing into a more technical data analytics role that combines problem-solving with hands-on data exploration.
